Recently, the UK Government mandated companies to publish stats on "Gender Pay Gap", showing the discrepencies between male and female pay. However, the term "Pay Gap" itself is misleading, as many companies with steep pay gaps respond by saying that they pay everyone the same way, and issues such as lack of female in senior management roles, unfair promotion practices, and systemic biases which are some of the root causes of the Pay Gaps get overlooked.
